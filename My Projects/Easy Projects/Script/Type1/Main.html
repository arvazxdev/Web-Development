What is Kotlin?

  • • Kotlin is a programming language.
  • • It is developed by JetBrains.
  • • Kotlin is a modern statically typed programming language developed by JetBrains. It is officialy supported by Google for   Android Development and is designed to be concise, expressive and interoperable with Java.
  • • Kotlin's demand on job market has been on the rise particularly in Android Development.
  • • According to the 2020 Stack OverFlow Developer survey, Kotlin ranked as the fourth most loved programming language.
  • • Major companies like Amazon, Netflix, Pinterest and Uber have adopted Kotlin for various projects.
